## How the Apprenticeship Works

The flow is pretty straightforward:

1. Build a profile as an apprentice (or a business/mentor)
2. Browse and connect — apprentices apply, mentors review and match
3. Start a 3-month apprenticeship: 10 hours/week from the apprentice, 1–2 hours/week of mentorship from the business
4. Complete the apprenticeship, get a certificate you can add to LinkedIn

The work itself varies by mentor. Some apprentices end up doing SEO audits and content creation. Others handle social media, paid ads, email campaigns, or website work. It really depends on what the business needs and what you want to learn — which makes it feel more like a real job than a classroom exercise.

One thing worth knowing: the basic apprenticeship for marketers is genuinely free. No catch. The business pays a subscription; the apprentice pays nothing.
